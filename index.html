Email

            Working of E-mail
The email refers to the electronic mail means of communication of sending and receiving messages over the internet. Email is the most common form of communication nowadays. An email has significantly evolved over the past couple of years. There are now stronger sync and messaging features along with stronger security and spam-related features.
Components of an E-mail
Sender: The sender creates an email in which they record the info that needs to be transferred to the receiver.
Receiver: The receiver gets the info sent by the sender via email.
Email address: The email address is just like a house address where the communication arrives for the sender and receiver, and they communicate with each other.
Mailer: The mailer program contains (and) allows the ability to read, write, and manage emails like Gmail, Outlook, etc.
Mail server: The mail server is responsible for sending, receiving, managing, and recording all the emails.

Protocols of E-mail:
Emails basically use two types of standard protocol for communication over the internet they are:
POP
POP stands for post office protocol for email. Similar to a post, our approach is just to drop the email over the service mail provider & then leave it for services to handle the transfer of message. We can be even disconnected from the internet after sending the email via POP.
IMAP:
Internet Message access protocol
IMAP has some special advantage over POP like it supports bidirectional communication over email and there is no need to store conversations on servers as they already will maintained in a database.
It has some advance features like it tells the sender that the receiver has read the email sent by him.
Sending an email:
Open the email client and click on the "compose new email" button.
In the "To" field, enter the email address of the recipient.
In the "Subject" field, enter a brief description of the email's content.
If there is a need to include an attachment, click on the "Attach file" icon and select the file to attach.
In the body of the email, type the message.
Once finished with composing, enter the email and click on the "send" button.
Receiving an email:
Open the email client and navigate to the inbox.
Locate the email to read and click on it.
The email will open, and the content can be read.
If there is an attachment, click on the attachment to open it or download it to the computer.
Forwarding E-mail
Open the email client and navigate to the inbox.
Locate the email to forward and click on it.
Click on the 'forward' button (usually an arrow) or select "forward" from the options menu.
In the 'To' field, enter the email address of the person to forward the email to.
If a message needs to be added to the forwarded email, it can be done in the body of the email.
If there's an attachment, it will be included in the forwarded email.
Once finished composing the forwarded email, click on the 'Send' button

Zoom

            Zoom and Google Meet are popular digital communication tools that allow users to conduct online meetings, webinars, and video conferences. These platforms heavily rely on Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) technology that allows voice calls to be made using an internet connection instead of a regular analog phone line. This technology facilitates real-time audio communication during meetings on Zoom and Google Meet. The use of VoIP technology ensures that both platforms can provide high-quality audio transmission, resulting in efficient and effective communication regardless of the participant's geographical location.
Steps to create and organize a meeting in Zoom or Google Meet for Zoom:
Log in to a Zoom account.
Click on the "Schedule a Meeting" button.
Fill in the details of the meeting, including the date, time, and duration.
Give the meeting a name and add a description, if necessary.
Choose whether to enable video, audio, and screen sharing.
Select the time zone of the meeting.
Choose whether to require a password.
Meeting Password or not
Click on the "Schedule" button to create the meeting.
Share the meeting link (or) invite participants via email.
When it's time for the meeting, open the Zoom app, navigate to the "Meetings" tab, and click on "Start" next to the scheduled meeting.

Google Meet

            For Google Meet
Log in to Google account.

Click on the "Save" button to create the event and send invites to participants.
